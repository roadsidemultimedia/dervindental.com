Trade in those Yellows for Pearly Whites

Depending on your bleach depth and your sensitivity tolerance, any of our teeth whitening systems can offer you a power-white celebrity smile. Choose between:

  • Our home-bleaching system — you receive a custom set of bleaching trays, tailored precisely to your teeth. We take a detailed set of impressions and modify them to hold our bleaching solution comfortably within your mouth; right against the cracks and crevices that need the most brightening.
  • Our ZOOM Advanced Power in-office treatment — a same-day treatment option that brightens your teeth anywhere from 5-8 shades in under an hour. We also provide the custom trays in the first option so that you can finish the blending phase and keep your teeth white for life.
  • Our deep-bleaching technique — is the most effective form of teeth whitening. We start with a shorter in-office conditioning session, followed by a couple of weeks of daily bleaching to increase the permeability of your enamel.

Once your enamel is ready, we conduct a full in-office ZOOM Advanced Power session and then apply a strong sealant that is especially effective for people with dark, dingy or tetracycline-stained teeth. One night per month you apply bleaching gel and wear your touch-up trays. That’s it!
